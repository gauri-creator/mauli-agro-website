Who We Are

Maharashtra's Premier
Turmeric Export House

Mauli Agro Industries is a family-owned agro export business founded by Nilkanth Kadam and Babarao Kadam, rooted in the agricultural heartland of Basmat, Hingoli, Maharashtra.

We source the finest turmeric varieties directly from farming communities across India — from Lakadong's high-curcumin hills of Meghalaya to Kerala's finger-rich Alleppey fields — ensuring farm-to-export integrity at every step.

Our commitment is simple: deliver the purest, most potent turmeric to global buyers while empowering Indian farmers with fair trade pricing and sustainable practices.

Our Journey

A Decade of Excellence

2013 – The Beginning
Founded in Basmat, Hingoli

Nilkanth and Babarao Kadam established Mauli Agro Industries with a singular focus — to bring Maharashtra's finest turmeric to national markets with full farm-to-buyer transparency.

2015 – Quality First
FSSAI Certification & Lab Testing

We tied up with NABL-accredited labs for batch-level curcumin and pesticide testing — setting a new quality benchmark that soon became our signature promise to buyers.

2017 – Going Global
First International Export

Our first international shipment of Lakadong and Alleppey Finger turmeric reached buyers in Singapore and Dubai — marking the start of our global journey.

2020 – Expanding Varieties
6 Premium Varieties Portfolio

We expanded sourcing to include Waigaon GI-tagged, Kandhamal Haladi, Salem/Erode, and Rajapuri — offering buyers a complete Indian turmeric catalogue under one trusted roof.

2023–Present
15+ Countries, Growing Strong

Today we export to 15+ countries across Asia, Europe, Middle East, and the Americas — with a growing network of farmer partners and a reputation for unmatched purity.
